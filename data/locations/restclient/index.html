  • info_outlineHow to use GUI

    You can search any field in the introduction of the payload. You can use "index" and "limit from page" to search for page, the "index" being the top of the page and "limit" as far show. So you can search by year from the initial date "from" until the end "to".

    Through the magnifying glass you can search by field you specify or leave empty to show everything. The red buttom can add a new item, the buttom blue can update an item from the list, and finally the black buttom can delete all the list or delete only the elements specify from of payload.
